Call students to school for Class IX, XI exams: Goa Board

PANAJI: The Goa board of secondary and higher secondary education has instructed schools affiliated to it that all exams for Class IX and XI must be held by calling Students to school. The move comes after the directorate of education's (DoE) instructions to start physical classes for these students. The board, however, has granted schools several liberties, including choosing the time, weightage and nature of the question papers. Institutes, for instance, can set their own examination papers, based on how much portion has been covered by the individual school in the pandemic-hit academic year.
